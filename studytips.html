How to prepare for finals

Tips and tricks to help you study for your exams

Posted by Study Buddy, December 5th/2018

Exams and finals seem like they come and go so fast. However, it can feel long and daunting in the process. It is important to be prepared, time manage, and take care of yourself during exam time. Below are some tips to help you get through the next month.

Tips for Finals

  1. Have a game plan: time management is key. Write down all of your daily, weekly, and monthly goals.
  2. Sleep and Eat:Please make sure to keep a healthy sleep schedule and dont skip on meals. Our brains need food and sleep to be fueled.
  3. Exercise:Exercise is a key factor in improving your well being, energy, and brain power.
